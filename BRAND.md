# Brand Guidelines

## Logo

The logo is located at `icons/logo.svg`. The icon is at `icons/icon.svg`.

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Red | `#ef4444` | Kebab chunk 1, logo letter "K", knob accent |
| Amber | `#f59e0b` | Kebab chunk 2, logo letter "e" |
| Teal | `#14b8a6` | Kebab chunk 3, logo letters "ba" |
| Purple | `#8b5cf6` | Kebab chunk 4, logo letter "b" |
| Skewer | `#404040` | Skewer stick, knob, pointed tip |
| Text dark | `#18181b` | "Tab" in logo |
| Tagline | `#a1a1aa` | Tagline text |

## Icon Anatomy

The icon is a vertical kebab skewer with four colorful chunks, slightly rotated for a playful feel:

- Chunk 1 (top): red, rotated -3 degrees
- Chunk 2: amber, rotated +2 degrees
- Chunk 3: teal, rotated -2 degrees
- Chunk 4 (bottom): purple, rotated +1 degree, slightly smaller

Transparent background. Chunks taper in width from top to bottom.

## Logo Text

- **"Tab"** — black (`#18181b`), Segoe UI, weight 900
- **"Kebab"** — each letter individually colored: K (red), e (amber), b (teal), a (teal), b (purple)
- **Tagline** — "Stack and organize your tabs", gray (`#a1a1aa`), weight 500
